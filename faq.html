FAQ

When is the game coming out?

We don't currently have an expected release date, but we'll let you know when the time comes.

What about copyright strikes or license issues?

We plan to develop this game as a game inspired heavily from Naruto, but not directly a Naruto game itself. Therefore, we plan to have original names and characters in our game, but the overall feel and look will be reminiscient of Naruto.

Will the demo's and final game be free for everyone?

Yes, it will always be free.

Will there be custom characters?

Yes, our team is hard at work on producing a custom-character creation system, so players can experience their own shinobi story.

Will the game release on IOS and Android?

With how development is progressing as of now, we do not currently plan for a mobile release.

How can I help?

If you have any useful skills, particularly Unreal Engine game dev experience, modelling, animating or graphic design then please do contact me through DM's on Discord. Aside from this, any support across my social media and patreon, or even invites to this Discord will really support my team and I.

Can you release it on console or mac?

We don't currently plan for console release, and mac is still unconfirmed but we're open to it.

Are you going to add story mode that follows the events in Naruto?

No, the game will not have a single-player story mode.
